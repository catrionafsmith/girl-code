Equality in tech has a long way to go, but why not start your own coding journey, promote tech industry roles to others, or empower your friends to take their first steps into coding?

Statistics on Equality in Tech

In 2022, the percentage of women in large tech companies sits at 25%. (Source: Deloitte)

Recent data showed that only 19% of students studying computer science related degrees were female. (Source: UCAS)

At the current pace of change it will take over 133 years until the economic gender gap is closed. (Source: WomenTech Network)

93% of the capital invested in tech companies went to all-male founding teams. (Source: Atomico)
